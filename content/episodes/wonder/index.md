What if the unknown wasn’t something to fear—but something to meet with
curiosity?

In this episode, we explore wonder as a powerful antidote to control, fear, and
self-importance. From “what’s the best that could happen?” to loosening your
grip on outcomes, we unpack how wonder opens new possibilities—in your choices,
your identity, and your capacity to experience life more fully.
